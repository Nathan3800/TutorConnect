Privacy Policy

At TutorConnect, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

  • Payment Information: Securely processed transactions via trusted third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is encrypted during transactions and is never shared without your consent.

4. Cookies

Our site uses cookies to improve user experience, track sessions, and analyze platform performance.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting our support team.

6. Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. We encourage users to review it regularly.
